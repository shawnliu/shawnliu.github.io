100 Postcard Paintings

I'm painting 100 paintings on postcard-sized paper and mailing them to friends and strangers around the world.

My only request: photograph the postcard when it arrives and send me the image. I will compile those pictures into a book.

If you'd like an original postcard painting in your mailbox, email me at postcard (at) this domain.
