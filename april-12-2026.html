Hello. I'm looking for participants to help me make my next zine.

On April 12, 2026, I'll be in Taipei, Taiwan visiting my mother. On that particular day I'll make observations and document them. I hope to make each observation a page, and I'm hoping to create at least 20 pages for that day.

I'm looking for at least twenty people to help me do the following:

  1. Choose a time between 8 a.m. and 8 p.m. On April 12th at that time, I'll document whatever I'm observing at that moment. It could be a screen, an object, a sentence, something I'm eating, or a sound I'm hearing. I'll document it and put it on a page. It could be a drawing, a collage, or a photograph. And I'll mail that page to you from Taipei.
  2. Respond to my page and mail it back to me. After you receive it, I'd like you to respond to it and mail it back to me within two weeks of receiving it. By respond, I mean anything — you can draw or write on the page, make a separate artwork as a response, or crumble or tear up the page.

That's it. After I receive your responses, I'll scan them and collect them into the final zine. I'll send you a copy of the finished zine, of course.

If that sounds like something you want to do, please fill out the short form below.
