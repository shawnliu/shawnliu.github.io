Hello! I'm looking for participants to help with a small art project in the tradition of mail art and one-day documentation.

On April 12, 2026, I'll be in Taipei visiting my mom. I'd like to document that day, and I need your help:

  1. Choose a time between 6 AM and 8 PM. Let's say you choose “1:23 PM.” At that time on April 12, I’ll take note of whatever I’m observing: a stray cat, a sentence, something I'm eating, or a sound I'm hearing. I’ll make something from that observation (a drawing, a photograph, a few lines of text) and mail it to you from Taipei.
  2. Respond to the page and mail it back to me in Brooklyn, NY. After you receive it, respond and mail it back to me within two weeks. By respond I mean anything — you can draw or write on the page, make a separate artwork as a response, crumple it, or tear it up.

I'll scan everything that comes back and assemble it into a zine. I'll send you a copy, of course.

If that sounds like something you want to do, please fill out the form below.
