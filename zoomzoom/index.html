Zoom Zoom Drawing Club is for anyone who wants to draw with other people, over Zoom. It really is for anyone––you can be a beginner who needs a little bit of encouragement, or a seasoned artist who just wants some company, or someone in between who just wants to draw. All levels are welcomed!

Each drawing club works like this: we gather, I'll share some optional reference photos (see examples below), and then we draw for half an hour quietly. After that there's a 15-minute session for those who want to share their drawings. No critiques allowed, clapping is encouraged.
